About Us

Welcome to De Lamps – customized lamps made just for you!

Founded on February 6, 2022, DeLamps started with a simple yet heartfelt idea: to create handmade lamps that bring warmth and personality to any space. Our first products were made by manually engraving designs onto acrylic sheets, paired with beautifully crafted wooden bases, all made by hand.

As our passion grew, so did the support from our customers across the Philippines. In 2025, we proudly reopened with improved tools and techniques, blending machine precision with the handmade quality that our supporters have always loved.

Today, DeLamps continues to serve clients nationwide, delivering quality, custom-made lamps that reflect both tradition and innovation. Whether for your home, a gift, or a statement piece, our lamps are made to brighten lives—one design at a time.

Thank you for being part of our story.
